# Privacy Policy for YouFit (یوفیت)

**Last Updated: November 29, 2025**

## Introduction

YouFit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Name**: Used to personalize your experience
- **Email Address**: Collected through Google Sign-In
- **Profile Information**: Age, gender, weight, height, fitness goals
- **Workout Preferences**: Equipment, location, training schedule
- **Health Information**: Injuries, focus areas (optional)

### Automatically Collected
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, workout completion
- **Authentication Data**: Google Sign-In tokens (Firebase)

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Personalized Workouts**: Generate AI-powered plans
2. **Authentication**: Secure login via Google
3. **Progress Tracking**: Save workout history
4. **Service Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns
5. **AI Generation**: Send preferences to Gemini AI

## Data Storage & Security

### Firebase Services
- **Firebase Authentication**: Secure login
- **Cloud Firestore**: Encrypted data storage

### Encryption
- Data encrypted **in transit** (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data encrypted **at rest** (Firebase)

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Sign-In**: Authentication
- **Firebase**: Data storage
- **Gemini AI**: Workout generation
- **Cloudflare**: Proxy for Iranian users

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share with advertisers
- Market outside the app

We **MAY** share with:
- Firebase (as described)
- Gemini AI (preferences only)
- If required by law

## Your Rights

- **Access**: Request your data copy
- **Delete**: Remove all data
- **Modify**: Update your profile
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using anytime

### Delete Account
Profile → Delete Account → Confirm

## Children's Privacy

Not for users under 18. We don't knowingly collect children's data.

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Until deletion
- Deleted: Removed within 30 days
- Inactive 2+ years: May be deleted
