Privacy Policy for YouFit (یوفیت)

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Introduction

YouFit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name: Used to personalize your experience
  • Email Address: Collected through Google Sign-In or email registration
  • Profile Information: Age, gender, weight, height, fitness goals
  • Workout Preferences: Equipment, location, training schedule
  • Health Information: Injuries, focus areas (optional)
  • Body Metrics: Weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass (optional)

Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version
  • Usage Data: App features used, workout completion
  • Authentication Data: Google Sign-In tokens (Firebase)

How We Use Your Information

  1. Personalized Workouts: Generate AI-powered workout plans tailored to your needs
  2. Authentication: Secure login via Google or email/password
  3. Progress Tracking: Save workout history and body metrics
  4. Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve the app
  5. AI Generation: Send your preferences to Gemini AI to create personalized workouts

Data Storage & Security

Firebase Services

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure login and user management
  • Cloud Firestore: Encrypted data storage for your profile, workouts, and metrics

Encryption

  • Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data encrypted at rest (Firebase)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: Authentication
  • Firebase: Data storage and authentication
  • Gemini AI (Google): Workout generation based on your preferences
  • Cloudflare: Proxy service for improved connectivity

Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Market outside the app

We MAY share with:

  • Firebase: As described above for app functionality
  • Gemini AI: Only your workout preferences to generate plans
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Remove all your data from our systems
  • Modify: Update your profile information anytime
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the service at any time

How to Delete Your Account

Navigate to: Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account → Confirm

Or contact us at the email below to request deletion.

Children's Privacy

YouFit is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained until you delete your account
  • Deleted accounts: All data removed within 30 days
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted
